Hyderabad To Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga

Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga is located in Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh, on the banks of the Krishna River. It is unique as it is also a Shakti Peetha, where Goddess Parvati is worshipped along with Lord Shiva. According to mythology, this temple was built where Lord Shiva and Parvati came to console their son, Kartikeya. The temple’s stunning Dravidian architecture and spiritual energy attract thousands of devotees every year, especially during Mahashivratri.

Hyderabad To Arunachalam

Arunachalam Temple, also known as Arunachaleswarar Temple, is a revered Shiva temple in Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu. It is one of the Pancha Bhoota Stalams, representing the fire element. The temple attracts devotees, especially during Karthigai Deepam, when a massive lamp is lit on Arunachala Hill. Surrounded by scenic beauty, it is a major spiritual and tourist destination. 🚩

Hyderabad To Tirupati

Tirupati’s Sri Venkateswara Temple, located in Tirumala, Andhra Pradesh, is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (Balaji). Hyderabad To Ujjain Mahakaleswar Jyotirlinga

Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga, located in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, is one of the most revered Shiva temples in India. It is unique because the Shiva Lingam here is Swayambhu (self-manifested) and faces south, unlike other Jyotirlingas. The temple is famous for its Bhasma Aarti, performed at dawn using sacred ash. According to mythology, Lord Shiva appeared here to destroy a demon named Dushana. Devotees believe that worshipping Mahakaleshwar grants protection from negative energies and fulfills desires. Ujjain, situated on the banks of the Shipra River, is also home to the Kumbh Mela, adding to its spiritual significance. 🙏🚩
